UTSAHA VAIBHAVA 2024

UTSAHA, an annual inter-collegiate techno-cultural fest organized by the students of BMS Yelahanka, Bangalore.
Started in the year 2004, growing bigger and better every year. BMSIT&M along with BMSSA is back with UTSAHA VAIBHAVA '24.
Giving students one of the grandest platforms to showcase their talent and bringing together students from different colleges all over Bangalore, attracting an estimated annual footfall of about 7,000 people, and boasts a number of competitive events, competitions and performances as well as pro-shows by widely known and popular artists.
